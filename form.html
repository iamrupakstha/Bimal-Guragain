About Me

Hello! I'm Bimal Guragain, an experienced English language specialist with a Master's degree in English. With over 8 years of teaching experience, I've helped hundreds of students achieve their desired scores in IELTS, PTE, and TOEFL exams.

My teaching approach focuses on identifying each student's strengths and weaknesses to create personalized learning plans. I believe in practical, results-oriented teaching that equips students with the skills and confidence they need to succeed.

Having worked with students from diverse backgrounds, I understand the unique challenges Nepali students face when preparing for English proficiency tests. My methods are tailored to address these specific needs.

My Teaching Services

Comprehensive English language programs tailored to your needs

IELTS Preparation

Comprehensive training for all IELTS modules with proven strategies to achieve band 7+ scores. Includes mock tests, personalized feedback, and time management techniques.

PTE Coaching

Specialized PTE Academic training with focus on computer-based test techniques. Covers all question types with practice materials and score-boosting strategies.

TOEFL Training

Targeted preparation for TOEFL iBT with emphasis on academic English skills. Includes integrated skills practice and test-taking strategies for all sections.

General English

Improve your overall English proficiency for academic, professional or personal growth. Covers grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation, and communication skills.

Writing Correction

Detailed feedback and correction for IELTS/PTE/TOEFL writing tasks. Includes band/score estimation, error analysis, and improvement suggestions.

Speaking Practice

One-on-one speaking sessions with personalized feedback and improvement plans. Simulates test conditions with experienced examiners.
